Documentation. Research. Identification.

We The People documents and analyzes online threats, groups, and digital behaviors. We investigate emerging patterns, maintain verified records, and identify coordinated activity across online spaces — protecting communities through research and insight.

Documentation

We collect and verify evidence of digital threats, misinformation networks, and organized behavior. Each record is preserved using forensic and open-source methods for transparency and accuracy.

Incident Tracking

Cataloging posts, interactions, and activities connected to misinformation or harassment campaigns.

Data Verification

All data undergoes authenticity checks, timestamping, and context validation to ensure reliable documentation.

Research

We study behavioral and network patterns across digital platforms to reveal structures of influence and coordination. Our research supports both defensive strategy and public understanding.

Behavioral Analysis

Tracing activity cycles, engagement patterns, and psychological levers used in online influence efforts.

Threat Intelligence

Assessing emerging risks, organized groups, and narrative campaigns that may impact security and trust.

Identification

We identify digital entities and behavioral signatures linked to harmful or coordinated activity — using ethical, privacy-conscious methods grounded in research and verification.

Digital Profiling

Recognizing recurring behavioral traits and affiliations across multiple online platforms and contexts.

Integrity & Ethics

Every identification process prioritizes transparency, accountability, and respect for privacy boundaries.
